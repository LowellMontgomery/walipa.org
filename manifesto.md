# Walipa Foundation: We Are The Living Planet

## Our Vision
To bridge the gap between advanced engineering and ancestral botanical wisdom.

1. **Plasma Infrastructure:** Developing in-situ mineral reclamation systems.

3. **Regenerative Land Trusts:** Protecting endangered habitats and traditional knowledge.
4. **Soil Restoration:** Utilizing mycology and permaculture to heal our landscapes.
