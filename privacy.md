# Privacy & Data Sovereignty

## No Tracking
Walipa.org does not use cookies, 
third-party trackers, or analytics 
scripts. We have no interest in 
harvesting your data.

## Identity Security
We encourage the use of security 
practices to thwart phishing and 
unnecessary data collection. We do 
not require legal identification for 
participation in our community forums.

## Data Usage
Any information shared within our 
Open Social community is used 
exclusively for coordinating 
stewardship activities. We will 
never sell or "platform" your 
personal information to 
third-party entities.
